This website has been developed as a comprehensive interface to a predictive and prescriptive model for dealing with the health and economic impact of Covid19. The OxfordCGRT data are fed into the model (or any other similar dataset), which can then work on a per country/region basis for arbitrary dates in the past and/or the future. This allows for comprehensive testing and optimization of different non-pharmaceutical Intervention Plan policies.
